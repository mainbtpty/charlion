Introducing Speech with Charlion: Advanced Text-to-Speech AI

Click HERE to launch Charlion Speech Synthesis App.

Speech with Charlion uses GPT-style models to generate audio from scratch. Different from Vall-E, the initial text prompt is embedded into high-level semantic tokens without the use of phonemes. It can therefore generalize to arbitrary instructions beyond speech that occur in the training data, such as music lyrics, sound effects, or other non-speech sounds. A subsequent second model is used to convert the generated semantic tokens into audio codec tokens to generate the full waveform.

Below is a list of some known non-speech sounds, but we are finding more every day. Please let us know if you find patterns that work particularly well:

  • [laughter]
  • [laughs]
  • [sighs]
  • [music]
  • [gasps]
  • [clears throat]
  • — or ... for hesitations
  • ♪ for song lyrics
  • Capitalization for emphasis of a word
  • MAN/WOMAN: for bias towards speaker

Getting Started with Speech with Charlion:

  1. Installation:
    1. Download the Speech with Charlion package from our official website.
    2. Extract the downloaded package to your desired location.
    3. Open the extracted folder and run the installation script.
    4. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation process.
  2. Using Speech with Charlion:
    1. Launch Speech with Charlion on the web by clicking HERE.
    2. Click on the "New Project" button to create a new speech project.
    3. Input the text you want to convert to speech or select a pre-existing text file.
    4. Customize voice options such as language, pitch, and speed.
    5. Click on the "Generate Speech" button to convert the text to speech.
